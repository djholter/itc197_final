Flight

medium: Earthenware Clay

dimensions: 6" x 11"

description: All vessels are formed on the potters wheel. Vases are partially dried, trimmed after which various tools are used to impress lines and shapes into the surface creating a spontaneous graphic design. Detailed painting with Terra sigilatta sets off the individual elements of the design. After the first firing a black copper wash is applied to entire surface. When dried the copper wash is wiped off leaving the copper only in the recessed areas. Earthenware clay.

Various sizes are available.
